About Author

One of the best-selling authors ever, Daniel "Dan" Brown has sold hundreds of millions of copies of his books. They have been translated into fifty-two languages. Born in 1964 in New Hampshire, Brown graduated from Phillips Exeter Academy and Amherst College. His hugely popular novel, The Da Vinci Code, was adapted into a film starring Tom Hanks and directed by Ron Howard. The same team took on the film adaptation of Angels & Demons. In In 2005, Brown was named one of the "100 Most Influential People in the World" by Time Magazine.
